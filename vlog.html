2019.11.22

Bangkok, Thailand

Day 1

We awoke in a new place and needing sustance. Walked to the smoothie bar across the street for smoothies. The owner knew no English, but we got a cheap smoothie to start the day.
Knowing not what to do, it must have been apparent on our faces. A man picked us out to explain that our current plan was no good (too many tourists). He grabbed us a tuk-tuk and sent us on an adventure.

Day 2

We took our own tour around Bangkok for day two. We walked through the Royal Park (Shan Saranrom) and saw some Komodo Dragons. We had some traditional breakfast at a local place. Visited the Siam Market for some sticky rice. Plus more.

Had breakfast at a place called 'The Tent', which was delicious and most western style we had in a while. We then went to visit the Phuket Elephant Jungle Sanctuary and made friends with elephants!

2019.12.05

Hanoi, Vietnam

Day 1:

We arrived in Hanoi after a long day in the airport (fuck Vietjet air, again). We had dinner at Garden House 2. It was a delicous meal after a hell of a day. Afterwards we walked around the backpackers district and smoked some shisha.

Day 2:

We woke up early to pack our stuff for an amazing cruise of Halong Bay. Indescribable.
Mon Cheri Cruises had an amazing buffet when we arrived to the ship. We got to see the Cave of Light and Dark. Later (during happy hour) we got to play some Sequence with our new friend Mo and learned how to make fried spring rolls!

Day 3:

We woke up a little late after the most amazing sleep and barely made it to breakfast. After our visit to another cave on Halong Bay we headed back to Hanoi via shuttle. After freshing up, we headed to Cau Go for a light snack and a great view of Hoan Kiem Lake. Next, we visited a beautiful temple on the lake with unfathomable history and had dinner at The Green Tangerine.Finishing the day with thirty cent pints.

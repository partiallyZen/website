Flight to Bangkok

SEA > ICN > BKK

Three hours to Seattle.
Eleven to Seoul Incheon.

2019.11.19
(Nov. 19)

Arrive in Bangkok

Hotel: The Royal Tha Tien Village

It was late when we arrived to the hotel. Exhausted we took a taxi to the hotel. Along the way we saw some beautiful architecture that forshadowed the beautiful time we would have. 
We then unpacked and went to sleep.

2019.11.20 
(Nov. 20)

Day 1: Bangkok

We awoke in a new place and needing sustance. Walked to the smoothie bar across the street for smoothies. The owner knew no English, but we got a cheap smoothie to start the day.
Knowing not what to do, it must have been apparent on our faces. A man picked us out to explain that our current plan was no good (too many tourists). He grabbed us a tuk-tuk and sent us on an aventure.

2019.11.21 
(Nov. 21)

Day 2: Bangkok

We took our own tour around Bangkok for day two. We walked through the Royal Park (Shan Saranrom) and saw some Komodo Dragons. We had some traditional breakfast at a local place. Visited the Siam Market for some sticky rice. Plus more.

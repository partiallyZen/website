Wat Phra Phiren:


This reclining Buddha can only be seen once a month and is largely a local temple. We met a man (with 3 girls) and his wife was praying for a boy to The Buddha.

Thai Export:


Standard Tourist Activities. We got sized for suits and shirts.

Phukhao Thong Thong/Wat Saket (The Golden Mount):


The Golden Mount was a beautiful temple and stupa. It was originally planned by King Rama III in...but that fell down and sank into the swamp. 

The second attempt was by Rama IV and was finished by his son.

See the gallery at the bottom of this page for more pictures.

Wat Inthawihan (Standing Buddha):


This Maitreya Buddha, or the Buddha of the Future, was an impressive 32 meters tall and 10 meters wide. 

We offered some prayers and toasted our bare feet.
